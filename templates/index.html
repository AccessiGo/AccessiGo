Welcome to AccessiGo!

A web app that highlights accessible building entrances around the University of Windsor.

UWindsor Campus Map (PDF) — click to expand

AccessiGo focuses on outdoor accessibility, showing which building entrances are wheelchair-accessible and clearly marking them on an interactive map. Future versions will expand to include other features such as indoor navigation, elevators, and ramps.
